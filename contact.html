FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your typical project lead time?

Standard CNC machining projects are typically completed within 2-3 weeks. Complex projects involving custom tooling or multi-stage manufacturing may take 4-8 weeks. We also offer expedited services with lead times as short as 5 business days for urgent requirements.

Do you offer design assistance?

Yes, our engineering team provides comprehensive design for manufacturability (DFM) analysis to optimize your designs for cost-effective production. We can also assist with CAD modeling, simulation, and prototype development.

What materials can you machine?

We work with a wide range of materials including aluminum alloys, stainless steels, titanium, Inconel, Hastelloy, engineering plastics (PEEK, Delrin, Nylon), composites, and exotic alloys. Contact us for specific material compatibility inquiries.

What is your minimum order quantity?

We welcome projects of all sizes — from single prototypes to production runs of 100,000+ units. Our flexible manufacturing systems allow us to be cost-effective at any volume. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific requirements.

Do you ship internationally?

Absolutely. We serve clients in 30+ countries with established logistics partnerships for reliable international shipping. We handle all export documentation, customs clearance, and can arrange door-to-door delivery worldwide.

About Stackly

Pioneering precision machinery manufacturing since 1999 with unwavering commitment to quality and innovation.

Our Story

Two Decades of Engineering Excellence

Founded in 1999 by a team of passionate engineers, Stackly Industries started as a small workshop with a bold vision — to manufacture precision machinery that could compete on the global stage.

Today, we operate from a state-of-the-art 200,000 sq ft facility, serving clients in over 30 countries. Our journey from a modest startup to an industry leader is driven by relentless innovation, an uncompromising commitment to quality, and deep respect for our clients' success.

We believe that great manufacturing is not just about machines — it's about the people, processes, and passion behind every component we produce.
